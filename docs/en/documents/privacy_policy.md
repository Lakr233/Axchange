# Privacy Policy

Axchange downloaded from the App Store does not collect any information.

Axchange downloaded from the official website may collect the following information for activation purposes:

- User email address  
- Device serial number  
- Operating system version  
- Axchange version  
- Activation code  
- Activation status  
- Activation time  
- Number of activated devices  

The above information is used solely for activation and deactivation verification and will not be used for any other purposes.

We may share this information with resellers and other third-party partners to verify the legitimacy of activation codes.
